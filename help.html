Welcome to the Calendar Reminder! Sign-in to your google account using the sign-in button at the top-right to add reminders to your calendar. Click on the 'Add Reminder' buttons located on the navigation bar or under the reminder panel. Check the 'Send email reminder' box in order to immediately email yourself the reminder. Once a reminder has been added, it can be viewed in the reminder panel. Additionally, hover over each month to see what reminders you have scheduled for particular months. In order to sign-out, the sign-out links (located in top-right or in footer of page) can be clicked.
